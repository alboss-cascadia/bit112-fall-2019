Website topic and title: Skeleton Residue

This will be a website for the punk electro-polka band, Skeleton Residue.

Target audience

Although Skeleton Residue is best known for performing the National Anthem of Fredonia during the World Cup Octofinals, they are actually Aruba's most popular punk electro-polka band, with an audience that consists heavily of women between 37 and 42 years of age, women in their late teens to early twenties, pre-teens of both genders, and males who are interested in impressing women between 37 and 42 years of age, and women in their late teens to early twenties--in other words, males.

Site purpose

This website will introduce Skeleton Residue to the handful of people who have not already been explosed to their sensuous wooing melodies. It will feature legal clips from their songs, as well as mashups with Skeleton Residue, Deathtöngueand The Vomiting Poodles. There will be frequent sales on merch and coupons for discounts on fresh fish.

Site personality

This will be a website that enjoys pterodactyls, wizards, cheese, and long walks to the kitchen to find cheese.

This website will be like a long walk on the beach with your lover, until the LSD wears off and you realize you're just dragging a stolen mannequin around a Wendy's parking lot.

At museums, this website would be allowed to touch the art. It could play a game of Russian Roulette with a fully loaded semiautomatic pistol, and win. Mosquitos will refuse to bite it, purely out of respect.

Limitations

  • For certain followers of Skeleton Residue, anything mechanical that they encounter refuses to work correctly. This could simply be mildly (-5) annoying – fuses that blow, constant static on the telephone line, or a VCR that eats the tape the very large neighbor loaned them. It could be a bit more (-10) serious – the electric sliding doors to the emergency room refuse to open for them, their car dies in the fast lane of a major freeway during rush hour, or they crash a world information network every> time they log on. Or it could reach catastrophic (-15) proportions – the entire city's phone bills are routed to their cell number, the IRS computer has messed their taxes up so often the local auditor now sends them birthday and Christmas cards, or they simply cause a two-square-block power outage wherever they go.

  • Spontaneous human combustion is a concern with followers of this band. If they are alone and sitting quietly, they have been known to burst into flames. If somebody comes into the room they can put them out by rolling them on the floor.

  • Some of this band's fans are dead. Finis. Kaput. Pushing up daisies. Gone to join the Choir Invisible. All attributes and skills are 0, except for odor, which is worse. They just lie there and rot. Their Appearance goes down by 1 every month unless they have been mummified or frozen.
